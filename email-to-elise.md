📚 **Your Book Shelf**
Log in and start adding books! You can search by title and it'll pull in the cover, author, and genre automatically from Google Books. Mark your favourites, write reviews, and rate them with custom labels: "not it" → "meh" → "solid read" → "obsessed" → "all-time fav"

✍️ **Writing & Poetry**
There's a whole section for your stories, poems, and journal entries. Anything you publish goes live on the public site!

🎨 **Art Gallery**
You can upload your artwork too — drawings, doodles, digital art, whatever you create. It'll show in a gallery for everyone to see.

✨ **Create Your Avatar**
Go to Dashboard → About and click the sparkle button on your profile picture. You can pick from 13 different styles — pixel art, robots, doodles, emoji faces — and keep shuffling until you find one that's SO you. Or upload a photo if you'd rather!

🎁 **Wishlist**
Add books you want to read (or want for your birthday 👀). Anyone can suggest books for you too!

📝 **About Page**
This is YOUR page. Add your bio, favourite genres, favourite book, fun facts, a quote you love — make it yours. It's at elisereads.com/about.
